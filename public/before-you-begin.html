Instructions for the conversation

You will now have a conversation with an AI chatbot about your beliefs on climate change. Please be open and honest in your responses. The AI is neutral, non-judgmental, and will not attempt to persuade you. The chat will last up to 10 minutes. Once only one minute remains or the chatbot has detected that the interview is complete, it will summarise your main points.
